About SAMUDRA

The Smart AI Monitoring for Unified Disaster Reporting & Awareness (SAMUDRA) provides ocean information and advisory services to support disaster risk reduction and ensure maritime safety for coastal stakeholders.

Our early warning services cover hazards such as tsunamis, storm surges, high waves, swell surges, and coastal currents, enabling authorities and communities to make informed decisions during ocean-related emergencies.

Platform Features

Geotagged Reporting

Submit geotagged reports, photos, or videos of observed ocean hazards via our platform.

Real-time Dashboard

View aggregated data on a dynamic dashboard with interactive maps and hotspots.

Social Media Analytics

Monitor social media feeds for hazard-related discussions and trends.

User Roles

Citizens

  • View hazard alerts and weather reports
  • Access interactive hazard maps
  • Upload geotagged hazard reports
  • Read safety blogs and guidelines
  • Receive emergency notifications

Analysts

  • Verify citizen-submitted reports
  • Monitor weather predictions
  • Analyze social media trends
  • Track government updates
  • Approve content for publication

Administrators

  • Issue emergency alerts via SMS/calls
  • Manage user accounts and permissions
  • Access comprehensive analytics
  • Coordinate emergency responses
  • Configure system settings
